Elements of Harmony, just like it’s name, is a Minecraft MOD, and, obviously, is inspired of My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic.

This MOD, which is build with Forge API, inserts six Elements of Harmony into Minecraft. These elements are:

  • Loyalty
  • Laughter
  • Generosity
  • Honesty
  • Kindness
  • Magic

(They are arranged in the order in My Little Pony Theme Song)

All we want to do is to make Minecraft as close as possible to Equestria, and Elements of Harmony is an essential part. It includes:

  • Six Elements, including their various forms.
  • Advancements of your life in Minecraft. (Not Featured)
  • A (or more) Dimension(s) for Equestria. (Not Featured)
  • Basic Support for further development. (Not Featured)

Yes, we’re still in progress, you can Watch, Star our repo on GitHub.
